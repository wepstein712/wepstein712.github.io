West Marches

This is a map generated by Dungeon Masters running different games during the initial beta test of the west marches.

What it is?

West Marches takes collaboration to the next level with collaboration not only between players but between DM's. West Marches is a shared adventuring space where DM's and players can come together without fixed groups or a fixed DM. The sessions are 'pick up' and you keep your character between sessions and DM's. Level up your heroes in the crucible of West Marches and fight everything from dire rats to dire lions to dire bears...oh my!

How to Join?

DM's will be posting about sessions in the facebook group. Just comment on the post, and show up and have fun! Remember to bring your character sheet! Look at the Useful Links for a guideline to create your character. At the end remember to look back to the post to find out what your loot was and how much XP you gained.

How to DM?

As a DM your first step should be to take a thorough read through the DM Guide in the Useful Links section. Then, figure out what you want to run, make a post on facebook, and run away (in the sense of run the game, don't run away, we are not liable for you running away from anything).

We have a link to the google drive folder with all the documents for playing in or DM'ing a West Marches game as well as other documents that might be important.
